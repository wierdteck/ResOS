# ResOS Demo Script (1-2 minutes)

## [0:00-0:10] Hook & Problem
**[Show Dashboard]**

"Restaurant owners are drowning in data. Menu prices? Set once and forgotten. Supplier costs? Constantly changing. Reputation? Monitored across different platforms. There's no single source of truth."

"Meet **ResOS**—the operating system for modern restaurants."

---

## [0:10-0:25] Feature 1: Menu Intelligence
**[Navigate to Menu page, show table]**

"First, intelligent menu management. Instead of guessing at pricing, ResOS links every dish to its actual supplier costs. 

**[Click on a dish to show Recipe Builder]**

You can build recipes ingredient-by-ingredient, choosing between your cheapest suppliers or locking in preferred ones. The system automatically calculates food costs, margins, and profit—updated in real-time as supplier prices change."

**[Show the profit/prep burden charts]**

"Instantly see which dishes are your profit drivers and which are prep bottlenecks."

---

## [0:25-0:40] Feature 2: Reputation & Supplier Management
**[Switch to Reputation page]**

"Second, consolidated reputation tracking. Reviews from Google, your website, and other platforms all flow into one dashboard. No more checking five different sites.

**[Switch to Suppliers page]**

And because supplier management is integrated, you can track pricing from multiple vendors in one place—making it easy to negotiate better deals and catch price creep early."

---

## [0:40-0:55] Feature 3: AI Insights
**[Return to Menu, show Analyze button]**

"Finally, AI-powered insights. Hit 'Analyze Menu' and get smart recommendations.

**[Simulate analyzing]**

ResOS tells you which dishes to promote, which to reprice, and which to simplify—based on profit margins, prep time, and sales velocity. It's like having a restaurant consultant in your pocket."

---

## [0:55-1:05] The Impact
"ResOS transforms fragmented data into **actionable intelligence**. 

Restaurant owners save hours every week on admin. They make data-driven pricing decisions instead of guesses. And they spot operational bottlenecks before they hurt profitability."

---

## [1:05-1:15] CTA & Vision
**[Show Dashboard overview one more time]**

"ResOS is built for restaurant operators who want to work smarter—by giving them visibility into the numbers that actually matter.

Ready to optimize your restaurant? Welcome to **ResOS**."

---

## Notes:
- **Pacing**: Speak clearly, pause between features
- **Visuals**: Smooth transitions, highlight key numbers/metrics
- **Energy**: Build momentum—problem → solution → impact
- **Length**: Flexible 1:00-1:30 range works for most hackathon demos
